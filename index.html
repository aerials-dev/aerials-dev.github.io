aerials

/ˈɛːrɪəlz/
adjective
•
Of the air; existing, occurring, moving, or operating in the air.
noun (plural)
•
Antenna systems designed to transmit or receive signals; structures that capture and relay communications across distances.
•
Views captured from elevated positions; bird's-eye observations that reveal patterns invisible from ground level.
•
High-flying stunts performed in mid-air; gravity-defying movements executed with skill and precision.
